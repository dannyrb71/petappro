Contrast targets — WCAG 2.1 AA (minimum)

1Body & small text: ≥ 4.5:1 against its background. Long-form: aim 7:1 (AAA).
2Large text (≥ 24px, or ≥ 18.7px at 700): ≥ 3:1.
3UI & graphics (icons, borders, focus rings, control edges): ≥ 3:1.

Type minimums

·Default body 15px. Never below 13px for essential text.
·Mono captions / eyebrows: min 12px at weight ≥ 700 — thin small mono is the #1 legibility trap.

Interaction

·Touch targets ≥ 44 × 44px (iOS) / 48dp (Android).
·Visible focus ring (3px, ≥ 3:1) on every interactive element.
·Never signal state by color alone — always pair with icon or text (all badges carry a label).
·Honor prefers-reduced-motion (built into the base CSS).
